Caribbean & The Bahamas

The Caribbean and the Bahamas are both popular tropical destinations known for their beautiful beaches and clear waters
Both destinations offer a variety of activities such as scuba diving, snorkeling, and fishing.
The Bahamas and Caribbean also have rich cultural histories, with the Bahamas boasting a blend of European and African
heritages, while the Caribbean has a diverse mix of cultures influenced by indigenous, European, and African traditions.
For cruises, a typical Bahamas itinerary usually only visits Nassau, the capital city, while a Caribbean cruise generally
includes multiple islands across the region.
